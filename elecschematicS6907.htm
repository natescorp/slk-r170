Trunk Lid "closed/locked" limit switch - S69/7 and S69/8.
Located in the left and right trunk wheelhouse, these switches connect a
ground signal through to the combination module (N10-3) as soon as the
trunk lid has reached the closed/locked position. The trunk lid
"closed/locked" limit switch (S69/7) is connected in parallel to the right
trunk lid "closed/locked" limit switch (S69/8).
