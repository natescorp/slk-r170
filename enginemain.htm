M111 2.3 Supercharged 2.3 Liter Engine

 M111 Engine
    - Tech Info

 

 

The M111 Supercharged engine employs an Eaton compressor having a capacity of approximately 1 liter per charge
cycle. Two triple rotors positioned at a 60 degree angle to each other rotate freely inside a housing, and deliver the
axial intake airflow to the opposing radial outlet. The supercharger is regulated by the hot-film engine management
unit, and is activated by a magnetic clutch when required. The intercooler has a great influence on the charging
efficiency. the engine is equipped with a new engine management system - ME-2.1. In addition to control unit
functions such as fuel injection ignition and anti-knock control, this also integrates functions for the electronic
accelerator pedal, drive authorization and cruise control.


    Engine: 111.447 - 4 Cylinder In-Line - 4 Valves per Cylinder - 2295 cc Displacement-
    Bore: 90.0mm -Stroke: 88.4mm-Compression Ratio: 8.8:1
