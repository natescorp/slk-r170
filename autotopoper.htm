Automatic Top Operation

 

 

 

 

 

(Click to view)

 

  

 

 

 

The Top Operating sequence is as follows:

All the side windows are lowered

The trunklid is unlocked and opened

The top is unlocked and retracted

The 2-piece hat shelf is raised, and
the cover sections at the sides are closed

The top is lowered into the trunk and
locked in place. The hat shelf returns
to its locked position.

The trunklid is closed and locked

The front side windows are raised

The rear side winows remain in their
recesses to eliminate the potential of
injury from exposed corners

If the car starts moving while the switch
is operated, the system depressurizes at
a speed of above 5 mph
